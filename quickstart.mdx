This guide walks you through the most common tasks on TruequeU. By the end, you'll have an account, know how to find items, and have created your first listing.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an account">
    Navigate to the **Sign Up** page (`/signup`) and fill in the registration form:

    - **Name**: your display name, visible to other students
    - **Email**: used to log in — must not already be registered
    - **Password**: must meet all of the following requirements:

    | Requirement | Details |
    |---|---|
    | Minimum length | 8 characters |
    | Uppercase letter | At least one (A–Z) |
    | Lowercase letter | At least one (a–z) |
    | Number | At least one digit (0–9) |
    | Special character | At least one (e.g. `!`, `@`, `#`, `$`) |

    <Tip>
      Choose a password you haven't used elsewhere. TruequeU stores credentials in your browser's local storage, so treat your account like any other web account.
    </Tip>

    Once submitted, you're logged in automatically and redirected to the home page.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Browse listings">
    Go to the **Listings** page (`/listings`) to see all available items.

    Use the **search bar** at the top of the page to filter listings by keyword — try searching for a textbook title, a course code, or a category like "electronics".

    <Note>
      Browsing is available without an account. You only need to be logged in to contact sellers, save favorites, or create listings.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save a favorite">
    When you find a listing you want to revisit, click the **heart icon** on the listing card. The icon toggles the listing in and out of your favorites.

    Favorited listings are persisted to local storage, so they'll still be marked the next time you open the app in the same browser.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Contact a seller">
    Open any listing by clicking on it to go to its detail page (`/listing/:id`).

    Click the **Contact Seller** button to start a chat with the seller. This opens a new conversation in the messaging section, where you can negotiate, ask questions, or arrange the exchange.

    <Tip>
      Be specific in your first message — mention which listing you're asking about and what you'd like to know. It helps the seller respond faster.
    </Tip>

    You can return to all your conversations at any time via the **Messages** page (`/chat`).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create your first listing">
    Ready to sell something? Go to the **Create Listing** page (`/create`) to open the listing form.

    Fill in the following fields:

    - **Title**: a clear, descriptive name for the item
    - **Description**: condition, edition (for textbooks), any relevant details
    - **Price**: set a number — remember, fair prices attract more buyers
    - **Category**: choose the category that best fits your item
    - **Photos**: add one or more images to help buyers know what they're getting

    <Warning>
      All listing data is stored locally in your browser. If you clear your browser storage or switch devices, your listings will not carry over.
    </Warning>

    Submit the form and your listing will appear immediately in the listings page with `available` status.
  </Step>
</Steps>
