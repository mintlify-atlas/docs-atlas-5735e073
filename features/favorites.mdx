---
title: Favorites
description: Save listings you are interested in with a single tap of the heart icon.
---

Favorites let you mark listings you want to keep track of. The heart icon on each listing card toggles the favorite state on or off.

<Info>Favorites are stored locally in your browser using `localStorage`. They are not synced to your account and will not carry over to other browsers or devices.</Info>

## How to favorite a listing

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find a listing">
    Browse to `/listings` or open any listing card.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click the heart icon">
    Tap the heart icon on the listing card. The icon fills in to indicate the listing is now a favorite.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Unfavorite">
    Tap the heart icon again to remove the listing from your favorites. The icon returns to its empty state.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## How it works

Favoriting calls `toggleFavorite(id)` in the Zustand store, which flips the `isFavorite` boolean on the matching listing:

```typescript
// Listing interface field
isFavorite: boolean;

// Store action
toggleFavorite(id: string) → void
```

The store is configured with Zustand's `persist` middleware, so the `isFavorite` state for all listings is written to `localStorage` and restored on page reload.

## Where favorites appear

There is no dedicated favorites page. Favorited listings are marked inline on listing cards wherever they appear — on the `/listings` browse page and on your `/profile` page.

<Note>To see all your favorited listings in one place, you can browse `/listings` and visually identify the filled heart icons on cards.</Note>

## Persistence behavior

| Scenario | Behavior |
|---|---|
| Page reload | Favorites are restored from `localStorage` |
| Different browser | Favorites are not available (stored locally) |
| Different device | Favorites are not available (stored locally) |
| Clearing browser data | Favorites are lost |
