## What is TruequeU?

TruequeU is a campus marketplace where university students can buy, sell, and trade items directly with each other. List your old textbooks, electronics, furniture, and more — or find exactly what you need from fellow students at fair prices.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an account">
    Sign up with your name, email, and a secure password. Your account lets you post listings, chat with other students, and save favorites.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Post what you have">
    Create a listing for any item you want to sell or trade. Add a title, description, price, category, and photos.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse and discover">
    Explore all listings from your community. Use the search bar to find specific items or browse by category.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Chat and negotiate">
    Found something you want? Click **Contact Seller** on any listing to open a direct chat. Agree on price and pickup details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the trade">
    Meet up, exchange items, and mark the listing as sold. Done!
  </Step>
</Steps>
